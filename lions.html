LION

The lion (Panthera leo) is one of the five big cats and a symbol of strength and courage. It inhabits sub-Saharan Africa and parts of Asia. Lions live in groups called prides and are second only to tigers in size among big cats. The species is considered vulnerable, with habitat loss and human conflict contributing to their decline.
