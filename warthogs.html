WARTHOG

Warthogs are wild members of the pig family found in grassland, savannah, and woodland in sub-Saharan Africa. Recognizable by their large tusks and wart-like protrusions, they are tough survivors, often kneeling to graze.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

You will find the warthogs in the savannah zone beside the watering hole.
